Discover the Beauty of the Western Cape

Your guide to the most stunning attractions in Cape Town and beyond.

Table Mountain Robben Island Cape of Good Hope Cape Peninsula False Bay

Table Mountain

Table Mountain is one of Cape Town's most iconic landmarks. Take a cable car to the top or enjoy a hike to experience breathtaking views of the city and the surrounding ocean.

Robben Island

Robben Island is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its deep history, including being the site where Nelson Mandela was imprisoned. Guided tours are available to explore its rich cultural significance.

Cape of Good Hope

The Cape of Good Hope is the southwesternmost point of the African continent. Known for its rugged beauty, it is a must-visit for nature enthusiasts and photographers.

Cape Peninsula

The Cape Peninsula offers scenic drives, stunning beaches, and diverse wildlife. Don't miss the chance to see the penguins at Boulders Beach or explore the Cape Point Nature Reserve.

False Bay

False Bay is renowned for its calm waters and family-friendly beaches. It's also a great spot for whale watching during the migration season.
